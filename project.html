What is this game :

In this game, you take on the role of a sorcerer tasked with defeating a group of magic-resistant knights to restore the glory of the wizards. As you play, you have the ability to launch attacks using various elemental powers. The knights wield shields that are vulnerable to specific elements, and choosing the right element allows you to inflict damage and earn points. After each playthrough, you'll receive rewards in the form of gold coins, which can be used to enhance your character's statistics and abilities.
